# Stronghold Brand Workshop Toolkit

Interactive brand discovery workshop for Stronghold Rescue & Relief.

**Live URL:** https://iambarabbas.github.io/stronghold-brand-toolkit/

## Features
- 4-session brand discovery workshop
- Auto-save progress to localStorage
- Manual save button
- Form validation
- Formspree integration for submissions
- Mobile-responsive design

## For Stronghold Team
Complete all 4 sessions at your own pace. Your progress is automatically saved in your browser. When finished, click Submit to send your responses to the Indigo Tones team.
